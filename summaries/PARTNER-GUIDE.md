## What is Try on Haul?

Try on Haul is a free virtual try-on app for festival fashion. Your customers can see themselves wearing your outfits before they buy—using AI that creates realistic photos in seconds.

**For retailers, this means**:
- ✅ Reduced return rates
- ✅ Increased conversion
- ✅ Free social exposure
- ✅ Trackable affiliate revenue

---

## How It Works

### The Customer Experience

```
1. Customer finds your outfit online
2. Pastes your product URL into Try on Haul
3. Uploads a selfie
4. Sees themselves wearing your outfit (with festival scene)
5. Shares to social media (with your product link)
6. Clicks through to buy from your store
```

**Time**: Under 90 seconds from paste to purchase decision.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to change anything on my site?
**No.** For affiliate/featured partnerships, we extract your product images automatically. No code required.

### How do you handle image rights?
Users upload their own selfies. Your product images are used under fair use for shopping/comparison purposes. We don't store product images—we link to them.

### What if someone shares a try-on but doesn't buy?
You still get free brand exposure. The share includes your product link, driving future traffic.

### Can I opt out?
Yes. Contact us to remove your products from Try on Haul. However, most brands prefer the free exposure.

### What commission rate do you require?
Standard affiliate: 5-10%. Featured partners: 10-15%. Negotiable for high-volume partners.
