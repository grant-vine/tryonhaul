## Target Audience

### Primary: Festival Fashion Enthusiasts

**Demographics**:
- Age: 22-35
- Gender: 70% female, 30% male
- Location: US, UK, Australia (festival culture)
- Income: $40k-$100k (disposable income for fashion)

**Psychographics**:
- Plans outfits for festivals weeks/months ahead
- Active on Instagram and TikTok
- Values self-expression through fashion
- Shops online but frustrated by fit uncertainty

**Behaviors**:
- Follows festival fashion accounts
- Saves outfit inspo on Pinterest/Instagram
- Shares OOTDs (outfit of the day)
- Attends 2-5 festivals per year

### Secondary: Festival Fashion Influencers

- 10k-500k followers
- Creates outfit content
- Partner potential for affiliate revenue
