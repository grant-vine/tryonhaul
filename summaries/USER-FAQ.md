## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Do I need to create an account?**  
Yes, you'll need to sign in with one of your social accounts (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or Apple). This is free and takes 10 seconds.

**Is it really free?**  
Yes! You get 5 free try-ons per day. More than enough to plan your festival wardrobe.

**What devices does it work on?**  
Any modern phone or computer with a web browser. Works best on iPhone and Android phones.

---

### Taking Good Photos

**What kind of photo works best?**  
- Stand straight, facing the camera
- Full body or at least waist-up
- Good lighting (natural light is best)
- Simple background
- Wearing fitted clothes (not baggy)

**Can I use an old photo?**  
Yes! As long as it's clear and well-lit.

**Can I use a photo of someone else?**  
No. You must only upload photos of yourself. This is in our terms of service.

---

### About the Try-Ons

**How long does it take?**  
Usually 60-90 seconds. You can wait on the page or leave‚Äîwe'll notify you when it's ready.

**Why does my try-on look weird?**  
Common issues:
- **Blurry face**: Use a clearer, higher-resolution photo
- **Wrong body shape**: The AI works best with front-facing, standing poses
- **Outfit not fitting**: Some complex outfits (lots of layers, unusual shapes) are harder for the AI

**Can I try on multiple outfits at once?**  
Not yet‚Äîone outfit per try-on. But you can generate up to 5 per day!

**Can I try tops AND bottoms together?**  
Currently, we support one garment at a time. Full outfit combinations are coming soon!

### Limits & Troubleshooting

**I hit my daily limit. When does it reset?**  
Limits reset at midnight UTC (check your local time zone).

**My try-on failed. What happened?**  
Sometimes the AI has trouble with certain images. Try:
- Using a different photo of yourself
- Using a cleaner outfit image
- Waiting a few minutes and trying again

Don't worry‚Äîfailed try-ons don't count against your daily limit!

**The app isn't loading.**  
Try:
- Refreshing the page
- Clearing your browser cache
- Using a different browser
- Checking your internet connection
